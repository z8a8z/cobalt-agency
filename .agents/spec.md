# Cobalt landing-page refinement specification

## Objective

Rework the public landing page around the new one-time pricing model while making its motion, mockups, bot, team cards, and Cobalt brand treatment more deliberate and substantially lighter to run.

## In scope

- Replace monthly subscriptions with the two supplied one-time packages and their annual maintenance renewals.
- Use Western/English numerals (`0`–`9`) throughout the page and embedded demo documents.
- Remove the sole audio asset and every audio-control path.
- Preserve the bot's hero laser, but remove button particle spray and nonessential bot detail/interaction work.
- Correct the mobile process-card rail and section scrolling transition.
- Remove mockup hover lifting, preserve usable manual QR-menu scrolling, and repair icon gaps in Cobalt demos.
- Apply the supplied Cobalt mark consistently in every first-party Cobalt logo treatment.
- Simplify known continuous animation sources, including the bot's decorative details and the Visit CTA map route.
- Update the team-card ordering and supplied role/Arabic role copy exactly.

## Acceptance criteria

- Pricing contains exactly Bio Link and QR Menu offers, each with a one-time price, included first year, and annual renewal price.
- A clear note says the page remains online even if renewal is late.
- No Arabic-Indic or Persian numerals remain in rendered first-party source content.
- No `callus.mp3`, `new Audio`, or music-particle implementation remains.
- Process cards fit at 360px and 390px without right-edge clipping or excess rail gutter.
- Direct manual scrolling inside QR-menu iframe content stays where the user left it.
- Phone mockups do not animate on hover.
- The Cobalt SVG geometry is used for the navbar, intro, footer, and Cobalt demo marks.
- The app builds and is checked at 360px, 390px, 768px, 1024px, and 1440px.
